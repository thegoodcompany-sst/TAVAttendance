## Page 1 — What we collect and why (plain-English summary)

**Who we are.** TAVA is a study centre run by **Talent Beacon**, a non-profit serving youth and
residents of Bukit Batok. Talent Beacon is responsible for your child's personal data under
Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).

**What we collect**

- **About your child:** name, date of birth, school, year of study, attendance and punctuality
  records, reasons given for lateness, exam result slips, dismissal / pick-up records, awards, and
  any notes our staff record about the Centre's programmes.
- **About you (parent/guardian):** name, contact number, your relationship to the child, and any
  messages you send us.

We do **not** collect NRIC or FIN numbers. The system actively blocks NRIC/FIN-style numbers from
being typed into notes.

**Why we collect it**

1. To enrol your child and manage class rosters.
2. To record and report attendance and punctuality.
3. To record exam results and academic progress.
4. To manage safe dismissal / pick-up.
5. To communicate with you about your child and Centre matters.
6. To meet legal, accounting and record-keeping duties.

**Who sees it.** Only Centre staff (tutors and administrators) who need it, and our technology
provider **Supabase**, which stores the data securely in **Singapore** (encrypted in transit and at
rest). We do **not** sell your data or use it for outside marketing.

**How long we keep it.** As long as needed for the purposes above and for legal record-keeping —
**up to 7 years after your child leaves the Centre** — after which direct identifiers are removed
and any retained attendance history is treated as pseudonymised data, or the data is deleted,
according to the DPO-approved retention schedule.

**Your rights.** You may ask to **see** the data we hold about your child, ask us to **correct** it,
or **withdraw your consent** at any time (see the withdrawal note on page 2). Contact our DPO.

### Withdrawing your consent

You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting our Data Protection Officer (details on
page 1), in writing (email is fine). When you withdraw:

- We will stop collecting, using and disclosing your child's personal data for the purposes above,
  **except** where the law requires us to keep certain records for a period of time.
- We will explain the likely consequences — usually that we can no longer provide the Centre's
  programmes to your child.
- We will confirm once your request has been actioned.

You may also, at any time, ask to **access** the personal data we hold about your child or ask us
to **correct** it. A reasonable fee may apply to an access request.
